CAD/CAM

DESIGN & MANUFACTURING

The CAD CAM course equip learners with knowledge in 3D modeling and manufacturing. The facilitators teach learners to model using software like Fusion 360, Rhinoceros 3D and SolidWorks. They model prototypes of products and buildings. This knowledge is crucial in the design and engineering space. They slice their designs and bring them out using the 3D printer, Laser Cutting machine and other small CNC machine. They model furniture and other amazing products.
